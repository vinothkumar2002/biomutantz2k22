ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

The Department of Biomedical Engineering offers B.E. (Biomedical) since 2019. The department has experienced and qualified faculty members, well equipped laboratories such as Biomedical Instrumentation lab, Diagnostic and Therapeutic Lab, Pathology and Microbiology Lab, Biochemistry lab, Human Physiology lab and Circuits lab with the availability of latest and updated version of software packages. The department has also promoted Center of excellence namely Center for Biomedical Research. The faculty members and students were the member of Professional society namely BMESI. The main objective of the department is to produce talented and versatile biomedical engineers to create innovative ideas in Health care and satisfy the social needs.

Investigating Neuroscience

The workshop elaborates on analysis of brain signals using Statistical Parameter Mapping a free and open source software written in MATLAB. In addition to standard M/EEG preprocessing, we presently offer three main analysis tools

  1. Statistical analysis of scalp-maps, time-frequency images, and volumetric 3D source reconstruction images based on the general linear model, with correction for multiple comparisons using random field theory.
  2. Followed by M/EEG source reconstruction, including support for group studies, EEG and sMRI priors
  3. Dynamic causal modelling (DCM), an approach combining neural modelling with data analysis for which there are several variants dealing with evoked responses, steady state responses (power spectra and cross-spectra), induced responses, and phase coupling.
